About Us!

Who are we?

We are Norwich's world class gym and training enterprise. We have been in Norwich since 2016, helping our visitors get FIT-NOT-FAT. Our team of health professionals and personal trainers are ready to work with you and for you every step and lift towards your goal body and fitness.

Our facilities!

We have a world class training gym and facilities which also include classes as part of your membership or for non mebers a pay per use fee - Please head on over to the facilities link above in the navigation bar, or just Click Here. to find out more.

The Café!

When you need a little boost, or a hearty lunch, our in-gym café can cater to all you needs. We have on sale a selection of sandwiches, paninis, and made to order food that will tickle your tastebuds and fill you with the energy to get through that last set!

We also have a large selection of hot and cold drinks to quench your thirst in and out of the gym, morning and night. Currently a special offfer is on - just say PRE DRINK to our café staff when ordering food and you will recive a free espresso shot with your meal!
