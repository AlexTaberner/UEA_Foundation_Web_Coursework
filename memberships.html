Membership Tiers

Bronze

The bronze level membership grants you access to:

  • Access to the gym in off peak hours.
  • Introductory session with one of our trainers.
  • 5% off at our café.
  • Exclusive discounts with our partners

£14 per month

Silver

The Silver level membership grants you access to:

  • Access to the gym anytime.
  • Introductory session with one of our trainers.
  • 10% off at our café.
  • Exclusive discounts with our partners.
  • One personal training session per month (1H).
  • Free access to additional charge classes.

£32 per month

Gold

The Gold level membership grants you access to:

  • Access to the gym anytime.
  • Introductory session with one of our trainers.
  • 15% off at our café.
  • Exclusive discounts with our partners
  • One personal training session per week (1H)

£55 per month
