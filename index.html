Healthy Eating

Healthy eating doesn't just have to be a pipe dream. We all want that perfect body and while you can spend your time in our world class gym, bulking your body out plus building your stamina on our range of equipment, that alone won’t lose you the pounds off your waist before Christmas!

To get that body you want you need to start looking at your diet and eating behaviour. Weight loss is 20% exercise and 80% diet, it takes willpower to stave off those hunger pangs for the uneeded calories, sugar and fats that we crave in our day to day lives.

Here at Fit-Not-Fat our personal trainers are there to help you on this journey in and out of the gym, with a range of diet/exercise plans to suit any lifestyle. Just pop in today and see what your body could be!
